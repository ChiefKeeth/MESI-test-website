Brand Voice

✓ Do

  • Direct and confident — state what MESI delivers
  • Technical but accessible — avoid pure jargon
  • Outcome-focused — lead with client benefit
  • Use industry-specific terms (mining, SCADA, payload)
  • Short sentences in headings; fuller in body
  • Southern Africa focus — "across Southern Africa"

✗ Don't

  • Vague filler ("world-class solutions"... alone)
  • Overly formal or corporate-speak
  • Exclamation marks in professional copy
  • Generic claims without specifics
  • Passive voice where active is possible
  • Mix tones — stay consistent across all pages

Logo Usage Rules

✅

Always use the logo on a dark background. The logo was designed for dark contexts. The minimum background darkness is #1A1A1A.

✅

Maintain minimum size. Full logo: min 120px wide. Icon only: min 32px wide.

✅

Use MESI-Icon.png for favicon, app icons, social avatars, and any circular/square badge placements.

❌

Do not recolour the logo. The gold and green gradient is the official colourway. Do not apply tints, overlays, or single-colour versions.

❌

Do not stretch or distort. Always scale proportionally.

❌

Do not place on light backgrounds without a dark container or overlay behind the logo.

❌

Do not use the slogan text ("Electrical · Mechanical · Design") as standalone copy — it is part of the logo only.
